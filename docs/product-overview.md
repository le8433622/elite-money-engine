# Product Overview

## Product name
Elite Money Engine

## Positioning
Elite Money Engine is a finance management SaaS for individuals, freelancers, and small teams. It helps users track cash flow, monitor budgets, receive alerts, and understand spending patterns through automation and AI-assisted insights.

## Core value
- Track income and expenses in one place
- Understand monthly cash flow quickly
- Detect risky spending earlier
- Use automation rules to reduce manual checking
- Turn raw transaction history into practical actions

## Initial target users
1. Individuals with regular income
2. Freelancers and solopreneurs
3. Small business operators with simple finance workflows

## Core product pillars
1. Finance tracking
2. Budgeting and planning
3. Automation alerts
4. AI insights
5. Subscription-based SaaS delivery

## MVP scope
- Authentication
- Transactions
- Dashboard overview
- Budget planning
- Automation rules
- Basic AI-style advice
- Billing-ready web product

## Business goal
Launch a usable SaaS product that can onboard users, convert part of them to paid plans, and expand later into advanced AI finance workflows.
