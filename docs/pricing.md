# Pricing

## Strategy
The initial pricing model should optimize validation and adoption before maximizing profit.

## Recommended plans
### Free
- single user
- limited transaction history
- basic dashboard
- limited automation rules

### Pro
- unlimited transactions
- budgets and goals
- advanced automation rules
- richer AI insights
- report export

### Business
- multi-user setup
- team visibility
- admin controls
- audit visibility
- priority support

## Suggested early pricing
- Free
- Pro: 99,000 to 199,000 VND per month
- Business: 499,000 to 1,500,000 VND per month depending on seats and features

## Launch recommendation
- start with Free and Pro first
- introduce Business once multi-user workflows are stable
- keep trial flow simple and easy to convert

## What users should feel they are paying for
- time saved from manual tracking
- financial clarity
- fewer missed spending issues
- automation and insight quality
